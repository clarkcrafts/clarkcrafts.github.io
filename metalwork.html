Custom Metal Designs

In the workshop in Sussex we work with steel & copper to make artwork for the home and the garden. Taking inspiration from nature we hand-craft a range of metal pieces from mild steel, stainless steel and copper sheet and bar. 

Practical

We believe that metal work should not only look good but also have a practical use, we make a variety of useful things for the home and garden.

Decorative

Whether you are looking for something for your wall or the garden, we have a wide range of metal art in our collection.

Unique Designs

Most of our designs and unique to Clark Crafts and we are continuously coming up with new ideas... have a look at the gallery below.

Rusty.... naturally

Our garden ornaments and plant supports are usually made from mild steel which will rust naturally over time

Hand Made

All of our work is made and finished by hand to order in our work-shop. We use traditional metalworking tools (and some not so traditional!)

Stainless Steel, Mild Steel and Copper

We make our art from marine-grade stainless steel, mild steel and copper.
